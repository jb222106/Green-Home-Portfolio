About Sustainable Home Appliances

Sustainable appliances are designed to use less energy and water, helping reduce environmental impact while saving money. These appliances are often certified with ENERGY STAR or other eco-labels that ensure they meet environmental performance standards.

Appliance Traditional Eco-Friendly
Washing Machine 100L water/load 50L water/load
Refrigerator 800 kWh/year 300 kWh/year
Light Bulb Incandescent 60W LED 10W
Dishwasher 6 gallons/load 3 gallons/load
Air Conditioner SEER 10 SEER 20+
Water Heater Traditional tank (inefficient) Tankless or solar-powered
Vacuum Cleaner High power, inefficient Low-energy, HEPA filter equipped
